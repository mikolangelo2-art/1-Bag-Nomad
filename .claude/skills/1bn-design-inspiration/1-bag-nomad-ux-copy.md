## Errors

**Payment failed**
> Something went wrong with your payment. Your card wasn't charged. Try a different card or check your billing details.
> *CTA: Update payment method*

**Offline / no connection**
> You're offline. Your trip is saved locally — changes will sync when you're back online.
> *(no CTA needed; passive state)*

**Sync failed**
> We couldn't save your latest changes. Check your connection and try again.
> *CTA: Retry*

**Something went wrong (generic)**
> That didn't work. It's on our end, not yours — try again in a moment.
> *CTA: Try again*

**Session expired**
> You've been signed out for security. Sign back in to continue where you left off.
> *CTA: Sign in*

## Confirmation Dialogs

**Delete a trip**
> Remove this trip?
> Your packing list and saved places will be deleted. This can't be undone.
> *Buttons: Remove trip / Keep it*

**Remove a packing item**
> Remove "[item name]" from your list?
> *Buttons: Remove / Cancel*

**Leave a shared trip**
> Leave this trip?
> You'll lose access to the shared itinerary and packing list.
> *Buttons: Leave trip / Stay*

## Localization Notes

- "Bag" may need to become "backpack" or "luggage" in some markets — keep it flexible
- "Tap the bookmark" → update to "Click the bookmark" for web/desktop
- Avoid idioms like "keep an eye on" for markets where that may not translate cleanly
- Allow ~30% character expansion for European languages in button labels
